The story behind Zandik's notes

Postado 16 de outubro 2024

Zandik a student at Sumeru Akademiya, it is not known for certain which darshan he was part of. Zandik's notes are a set of interactive items that can be found throughout the Sumero region, in the forest and in the desert. In them we have the student's notes during expeditions. There is also a note from Soreh, a member of the Amurta. Your note is linked directly with zandik notes.
